About the Lunar Research Initiative

The Lunar Research Initiative (LRI) develops quality, reliability, and manufacturing frameworks for future lunar infrastructure. The goal is simple: give mission planners, engineers, and regulators a set of tools they can trust when they design factories and construction systems on the Moon.

Our Focus

Quality Engineering

Translating proven quality methods from aerospace and medical devices into lunar surface operations.

Regolith Manufacturing

Studying how regolith behaves as a production material and how to keep those processes stable over time.

Predictive and Risk Based Methods

Building architectures that connect CTQs, capability, reliability, and mission risk in one structure.
