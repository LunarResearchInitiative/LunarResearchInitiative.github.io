Partnerships

LRI collaborates with agencies, universities, and companies that want to mature the quality foundations of lunar infrastructure. Partnerships range from joint research to method reviews and training.

Agencies and Programs

Supporting mission assurance teams as they evaluate concepts for lunar construction and production.

Universities and Labs

Connecting academic regolith and autonomy research to practical quality and reliability structures.

Industry Collaborations

Working with startups and established companies on architectures, test strategies, and risk models.
