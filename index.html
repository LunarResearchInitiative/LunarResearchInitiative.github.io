Engineering the Quality Foundations of Lunar Exploration

Advancing lunar quality systems, evidence integrity, verification logic, and decision assurance for safe, reliable, and mission-ready operations.

Explore Our Research Partner With LRI

Lunar Research Initiative is a scientific and educational organization focused on the research, standards, and practical tools required for sustained lunar infrastructure.

Most failures in complex systems do not begin as obvious defects. They begin as timing problems, evidence gaps, configuration drift, and decision mismatches that look manageable until operating conditions expose them.

LRI studies those failure pathways and develops frameworks that help engineers, leaders, and researchers determine what the evidence can support, when verification remains valid, and who should hold decision authority under delay and uncertainty.
