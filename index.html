What is the Lunar Research Initiative

The Lunar Research Initiative (LRI) develops the scientific foundations that future lunar missions need to build safe and reliable infrastructure on the Moon. We combine quality engineering, reliability science, and lunar manufacturing research to create methods that mission planners can trust.

Featured Publication

Governance Beyond Earth: Decision Authority, Verification, and Evidence Integrity for Lunar Operations

Book Published

This book examines governance failure modes unique to lunar and deep-space operations, where delayed authority, degraded evidence, and constrained verification challenge terrestrial decision practices. It provides a technical foundation for decision authority, evidence integrity, and verification logic under operational latency.
