Cite LRI

If you reference LRI website content, please cite the site. If you reference a specific paper, please cite the DOI shown on that publication record.

Recommended citation

APA: Lunar Research Initiative. (2025). Lunar Research Initiative. https://lunarresearchinitiative.github.io/

IEEE: Lunar Research Initiative, “Lunar Research Initiative,” 2025. [Online]. Available: https://lunarresearchinitiative.github.io/. [Accessed: Month Day, Year].

BibTeX

@misc{LRI_website_2025,
  author       = {Lunar Research Initiative},
  title        = {Lunar Research Initiative},
  year         = {2025},
  howpublished = {\url{https://lunarresearchinitiative.github.io/}},
  note         = {Accessed: Month Day, Year}
}

Citing publications

For preprints and published papers, cite the DOI on the publication record. You can find all DOIs on the Publications page.
