LRI Initiatives

LRI runs focused initiatives that turn research ideas into practical tools, handbooks, and open references for the lunar community.

ULQF Handbook Series

Handbooks that describe a unified quality architecture for lunar production and infrastructure systems.

Lunar Risk Management System (LRMS)

A structured approach for mapping mission risk, quality controls, and operational constraints.

Regolith Manufacturing Playbooks

Guides for commissioning, validating, and monitoring regolith based production lines.

Aerospace Quality Brief

A weekly newsletter that shares lessons from aerospace and medtech quality and applies them to space.
