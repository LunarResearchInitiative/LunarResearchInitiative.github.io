Publications

The Lunar Research Initiative publishes open-access technical papers that advance quality engineering, regolith manufacturing, predictive analytics, risk-based architectures, and mission assurance for lunar surface operations. All publications are freely available for researchers, mission planners, and industry.

For reviewers and editors: LRI publications are presented as applied research contributions addressing quality, risk, and manufacturing readiness challenges in lunar and autonomous systems. All papers are released as open-access preprints to support early technical review, cross-disciplinary feedback, and standards-aligned discussion prior to or alongside formal journal publication.
