Temporal Misalignment Under Delayed Supervision

The latest LRI journal article examines how delayed supervision can create a safety-relevant mismatch between current lunar operating conditions and the evidence available to remote decision-makers.

Published

Temporal Misalignment Under Delayed Supervision in Lunar Surface Operations

Lunar hazards can change faster than delayed ground supervision can observe, interpret, and respond. This article examines temporal validity, evidence aging, local operating authority, and the risk of decisions based on an outdated operational picture.

Author: Ralph Figueroa
Journal: Journal of Space Safety Engineering
Year: 2026
Type: Peer-reviewed journal article
